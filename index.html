    How can I get a proposal?

    Please contact us here with a first summary/overview of your project or a RFI/FRP. We aim to reply within 24 hours to either schedule a first introductory call or ask any immediate questions we might have. During the call, we will ask questions regarding project goals, target audience, budget and timeline & further discuss the next steps together with you. We do not mind signing an NDA, but we assure to treat all client information confidential. After our call, we discuss your project internally with all involved people (e.g. designer, developers, project managers) and email you a proposal with a description of our approach, project phases, team composition and timeline.

    Do you provide only design for my project, or development as well?

    We usually advice to build the design and development from a single source. During the past years, we have perfected the way our designers and developers work together to provide the most efficient output. Although we love to be involved in all steps along the way, we also provide design-only/development-only work for certain projects. Please get in touch with us to discuss your project.
